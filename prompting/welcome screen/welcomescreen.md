# Welcome Screen Design System - Common Elements

## Layout Structure

All welcome screens follow a vertical stack layout with these sections from top to bottom:

1. **Main Content Area** (top-middle, takes most space)
2. **Action Buttons** (below content)
3. **Legal Text** (small, below buttons)
4. **Footer Bar** (bottom, dark background)

---

## Common Components

### Main Visual Area
- Position: Upper-middle section, centered
- Contains: Large illustration, character, or logo
- Purpose: Brand identity and visual interest
- Background: Transparent (shows gradient background)

### Brand Name/Logo
- Position: Below main visual, centered
- Style: Large, bold text or logo graphic
- Color: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White on gradient background
- Size: Prominent, eye-catching

### Tagline/Headline
- Position: Below brand name
- Style: Medium-sized text, 1-2 lines
- Color: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White on gradient background
- Purpose: Describes app value proposition

### Primary Button
- Position: Below tagline, centered
- Style: Solid colored rectangle with rounded corners
- Background: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White
- Text: `primary` (#6B46C1) - Deep purple, bold, centered
- Label: "Get Started", "Create account", "Let's get started"
- Size: Full width with side margins, medium height

### Secondary Button
- Position: Below primary button, centered
- Style: Outlined rectangle with rounded corners
- Background: Transparent
- Border: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White border
- Text: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White text
- Label: "I already have an account", "Sign in", "Log in"
- Size: Same as primary button

### Legal Text
- Position: Below secondary button
- Style: Small text, centered
- Color: `lavenderLight` (#E9D5FF) - Light purple/lavender for subtle contrast
- Content: "By continuing, you agree to..." with underlined links
- Links: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White, underlined

### Footer Bar
- Position: Bottom of screen, full width
- Background: Black (#000000) - Matches gradient bottom
- Height: Short bar
- Left side: Small brand icon + brand name in `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF)
- Right side: "curated by" text + Mobbin logo in `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF)
- Purpose: Attribution/watermark

---

## Color Patterns

### Background
- **Screen background**: Gradient from `primary` (#6B46C1) at top to black (#000000) at bottom
- **Gradient direction**: Vertical, top to bottom
- **Gradient stops**: Deep purple at top transitioning smoothly to black at bottom

### Button Colors
- **Primary button**: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White, solid fill
- **Primary button text**: `primary` (#6B46C1) - Deep purple text
- **Secondary button**: Transparent with `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) border
- **Secondary button text**: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White text

### Text Colors
- **Brand name**: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White on gradient background
- **Tagline**: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White on gradient background
- **Legal text**: `lavenderLight` (#E9D5FF) - Light purple/lavender for subtle contrast
- **Links**: `backgroundWhite` (#FFFFFF) - White, underlined

---

## Spacing Patterns

- Large gap between visual and brand name
- Medium gap between brand name and tagline
- Large gap between tagline and buttons
- Small gap between primary and secondary buttons
- Medium gap between buttons and legal text
- Footer bar sits at very bottom with no gap

---

## Typography Patterns

- **Brand name**: Largest text, bold weight
- **Tagline**: Medium size, regular weight
- **Button text**: Medium size, bold/semibold weight
- **Legal text**: Smallest text, regular weight

---

## Common Layout Principles

1. **Centered alignment**: All content centered horizontally
2. **Vertical stacking**: Elements flow top to bottom
3. **Breathing room**: Generous spacing between sections
4. **Visual hierarchy**: Brand name largest, buttons prominent, legal text smallest
5. **Full-width buttons**: Buttons span most of screen width with side margins
6. **Rounded corners**: All buttons have rounded corners (not sharp rectangles)
