## 2. Target Audience (Landing Page Messaging)

**Primary Audience:**
- Couples of any gender or sexuality
- Early, mid, or long-term relationships
- Sex-positive but need easier communication
- Want judgment-free exploration
- Seeking to discover new shared interests

**Messaging Tone:**
- Playful, warm, and intimate
- Sex-positive but not pornographic
- Judgment-free and shame-free
- Trustworthy and secure
- Approachable, not clinical

---

## 3. Key Problems to Address

**Primary Pain Points to Highlight:**
1. Talking about sex preferences feels awkward, risky, or shame-inducing
2. Partners fear negative judgment or rejection
3. Couples struggle to introduce novelty or discuss boundaries safely
4. Hard to know where to start or what language to use

**Solutions to Emphasize:**
- Low-pressure, private discovery first
- No awkward conversations required
- Clear boundaries and shared interests revealed
- Safe space for exploration

## 4. Landing Page Sections

### Hero Section
**Must Include:**
- Compelling headline that addresses the core problem
- Subheadline explaining the solution
- Primary CTA: "Download for iOS" / "Download for Android" / "Get Started"
- Secondary CTA: "Learn More" or "See How It Works"
- Hero visual: App mockup or illustration (suggestive but not explicit)
- Trust signal: "100% Private" or "End-to-End Encrypted"

**Headline Options:**
- "Finally, a way to talk about what you both want"
- "Discover your shared desiresâ€”without the awkward conversation"
- "Swipe through intimacy topics. Match with your partner. Explore together."

### How It Works Section
**3-Step Process:**
1. **Discover Privately** - Swipe through intimacy topics at your own pace (Super Yum, Yum, Open, or Ick)
2. **Share What You Want** - Choose what level of detail to share with your partner
3. **See Your Matches** - Discover shared interests, boundaries, and opportunities for exploration

**Visual:** Simple illustrations or app screenshots showing the swipe interface

### Key Features Section
**Features to Highlight:**
- **Private Discovery** - Explore your interests without judgment
- **Partner Matching** - Compare profiles to find shared interests and boundaries
- **Privacy First** - End-to-end encrypted, zero contact syncing, full data control
- **AI Suggestions** - Personalized ideas based on your shared interests
- **Educational Content** - Non-judgmental articles and guides

**Format:** Feature cards with icons/illustrations

### Trust & Privacy Section
**Trust Signals:**
- End-to-end encrypted partner sharing
- Zero contact syncing
- Full data deletion on request
- Your data belongs to you
- App Store approved content

**Visual:** Security badges or icons

### Pricing Section
**Freemium Model:**
- **Free Tier:** Personal discovery journey, basic content, 1 saved profile
- **Premium (Couple Bundle):** Partner matching, AI suggestions, specialist content, weekly prompts, relationship insights

**Marketing Angle:** "Perfect anniversary / Valentine's / 'spice it up' gift"

**Visual:** Pricing cards with clear feature comparison

### Social Proof Section
**Include:**
- Testimonials (if available)
- "As featured in" media mentions
- User count or relationship impact stats
- Trust badges

### Final CTA Section
**Strong Call-to-Action:**
- "Start Your Journey Together"
- "Download Peek Now"
- "Get Started Free"

**Include:** App Store and Google Play badges

### Footer
- Privacy Policy link
- Terms of Service link
- Contact/Support
- Social media links (if applicable)

## 9. Content Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use inclusive language (all genders, sexualities)
- Emphasize privacy and security
- Focus on relationship benefits
- Use warm, approachable tone
- Highlight ease of use

**Don't:**
- Use clinical or medical language
- Be explicit or graphic
- Make assumptions about users
- Use fear-based messaging
- Overcomplicate the value prop

## 10. Success Criteria

**Landing Page is Successful If:**
- Clear value proposition understood in < 10 seconds
- Users can explain what Peek does after viewing
- High click-through rate to app stores
- Low bounce rate (< 50%)
- Positive first impression (trustworthy, approachable)
- Mobile experience is excellent
