## üó£Ô∏è Voice & Tone

### Core Voice Pillars

1. **Playful but Mature**
   - Light, cheeky, and fun
   - Adult and modern, never juvenile or crude
   - Witty without being inappropriate

2. **Approachable & Non-Clinical**
   - Warm and welcoming
   - Avoid therapy jargon
   - Avoid porn-like language
   - Conversational, like talking to a trusted friend

3. **Shame-Free**
   - Normalize exploration
   - Reduce awkwardness
   - Emphasize mutual consent and comfort
   - No judgment, ever

4. **Private & Trustworthy**
   - Safe and confidential
   - Professional but not corporate
   - Reassuring without being patronizing

5. **Couples-First**
   - Focus on connection and discovery
   - Emphasize playfulness between established partners
   - Celebrate mutual exploration

## üé® Visual Identity

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- **Soft Coral** `#FF8A80` - Warm, playful, inviting (primary CTA, "Yum" actions)
- **Gentle Mint** `#81C784` - Fresh, positive, safe (success states, "Yum" accent)
- **Dusty Rose** `#F48FB1` - Romantic, soft, approachable (secondary accents)

**Neutral Colors:**
- **Warm Gray** `#616161` - Primary text, professional but not cold
- **Light Gray** `#E0E0E0` - Backgrounds, subtle dividers
- **Off-White** `#FAFAFA` - Card backgrounds, clean spaces

**Action Colors:**
- **"Yum" Green** `#66BB6A` - Positive, affirming, go-ahead
- **"Ick" Red** `#EF5350` - Clear rejection, but not harsh (softer than pure red)
- **"Maybe" Amber** `#FFB74D` - Neutral, contemplative (if implemented)

**Accessibility:**
- All text meets WCAG AA contrast ratios
- Color is never the only indicator (use icons + text)

### Typography

**Primary Font (App):**
- **System Font Stack** (San Francisco on iOS, Roboto on Android)
  - Clean, modern, highly readable
  - Familiar to users (reduces cognitive load)
  - Optimized for mobile screens

**Display/Headings:**
- **Bold weight** for emphasis
- **Medium weight** for subheadings
- **Regular weight** for body text

**Font Sizes:**
- **H1/Title:** 28-32px (bold)
- **H2/Subtitle:** 22-24px (medium)
- **Body:** 16-18px (regular)
- **Small/Caption:** 14px (regular)
- **Button Text:** 16-18px (medium/semibold)

### Iconography & Imagery

**Style:**
- **Abstract, couple-friendly, non-explicit**
- Soft, rounded shapes (no sharp edges)
- Minimalist line art or subtle illustrations
- Avoid literal representations of sexual acts
- Use symbols of connection: overlapping circles, linked elements, playful abstractions

**Imagery Guidelines:**
- No photography of people (maintains privacy, avoids assumptions)
- Abstract patterns, gradients, or geometric shapes
- Warm, inviting color washes
- Subtle textures that feel premium but not clinical

**Icon Examples:**
- Heart shapes (abstract, not literal)
- Overlapping circles (connection, partnership)
- Playful geometric patterns
- Soft, rounded abstract forms

## ‚úÖ Do's and Don'ts

### ‚úÖ DO

- Use warm, inviting language
- Keep it simple and conversational
- Emphasize safety and privacy
- Focus on couples and connection
- Use playful but mature tone
- Normalize exploration
- Make it feel like a game, not therapy
- Use "we" and "together" language
- Be reassuring about privacy
- Keep copy concise (mobile-first)

### ‚ùå DON'T

- Use clinical or therapy language
- Use explicit or porn-like language
- Make assumptions about preferences
- Use singles/dating app language
- Be preachy or judgmental
- Over-explain or be verbose
- Use corporate jargon
- Make it feel like homework
- Be too serious or clinical
- Use gendered assumptions
